Product Variations

The databases record seven purchases of twine. Two of these are qualified as 'sail' twine, two are qualified as 'soft' twine. These four purchases are priced by weight, the remaining three purchases with no qualifiers are measured by the hank. The prices for twine range from eight pence for a hank of twine to one shilling eighteen pence for a pound of soft twine.
